Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our Tally services

Do you offer Tally customization for specific industries?

Yes! We specialize in tailoring Tally solutions for various industries including retail, manufacturing, healthcare, education, and professional services. Our team understands the unique accounting needs of different sectors.

How long does Tally implementation take?

Implementation typically takes 2-5 days depending on your business size and complexity. Small businesses can often be set up in 1-2 days, while larger enterprises with complex requirements may need up to a week.

Is remote support available?

Absolutely! We assist clients worldwide via Zoom, TeamViewer, and other remote support tools. Our team is available across multiple time zones to provide timely assistance whenever you need it.

What if we need training for our staff?

We provide comprehensive training packages tailored to your team's skill levels. Training can be conducted on-site or remotely, and we offer follow-up sessions to ensure your staff is comfortable with the system.

How do you handle data security?

Data security is our top priority. We implement industry-standard encryption for all data transfers, conduct regular backups, and follow strict access control protocols. For cloud implementations, we use only certified data centers with robust security measures.
